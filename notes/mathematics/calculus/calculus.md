## 单变量微分

*1*    
求 $\lim\limits_{x\rightarrow0^+}\left(\cos\sqrt{x}\right)^{1/x}$

->
<div class="anstxt">
解：

$$
\begin{aligned}
\lim_{x\rightarrow0^+}\left(\cos\sqrt{x}\right)^{1/x} &=
\lim_{x\rightarrow0^+}\left(1+\cos\sqrt{x}-1\right)^{\frac{1}{\cos\sqrt{x}-1}\cdot\frac{\cos\sqrt{x}-1}{x}}\\
&= \mathrm{e}^{-1/2}
\end{aligned}
$$
